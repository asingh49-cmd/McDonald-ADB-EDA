## McDonald’s All-Day Breakfast Promotion Analysis

**Objective:**  
Evaluate the performance of McDonald’s **All-Day Breakfast** promotion in the **Michigan region** to determine whether it should be **continued, modified, or discontinued**.

**Approach:**
- Collected and prepared sales and regional data related to the promotion.  
- Conducted **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** and built **interactive Tableau dashboards** to visualize sales trends and performance by time, product, and region.  
- Compared performance metrics before and after the promotion launch.  
- Identified trends in consumer preferences and profitability impact.

**Tools & Techniques:**  
`Tableau`, `Excel`, `Data Visualization`, `KPI Analysis`, `Comparative Analysis`
